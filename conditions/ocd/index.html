Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder

Learn about OCD symptoms, daily life impact, and evidence-based treatment approaches at Westwood Institute.

About OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder involves intrusive obsessions and compulsive rituals that can become distressing and time consuming.

Common Symptoms

Symptoms can include checking, contamination fears, symmetry concerns, intrusive thoughts, reassurance seeking, and mental compulsions.

Treatment Approach

Treatment may include exposure and response prevention, cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness-informed strategies, and individualized planning.
