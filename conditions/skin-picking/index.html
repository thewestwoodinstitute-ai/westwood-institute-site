Skin Picking

Learn about excoriation disorder symptoms and treatment approaches at Westwood Institute.

About Skin Picking

Skin picking can involve repetitive picking that causes tissue damage, distress, or functional impairment.

Common Symptoms

Symptoms may include urges, repetitive picking, scanning, attempts to conceal marks, and difficulty stopping.

Treatment Approach

Treatment may include awareness work, habit reversal strategies, trigger management, and individualized support.
