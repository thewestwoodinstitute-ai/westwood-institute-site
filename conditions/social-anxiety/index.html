Social Anxiety

Learn about social anxiety symptoms and treatment approaches at Westwood Institute.

About Social Anxiety

Social anxiety can involve intense fear of judgment, embarrassment, or scrutiny in social or performance situations.

Common Symptoms

Symptoms may include avoidance, anticipatory anxiety, self-monitoring, and distress in social settings.

Treatment Approach

Treatment helps patients reduce avoidance and build tolerance for feared social situations.
